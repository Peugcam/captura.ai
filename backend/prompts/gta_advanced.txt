🎯 CHALLENGE: Players customize their UI, so:
- Kill feed position varies (top-right, top-left, center, bottom)
- Colors vary (white, red, yellow, cyan, custom RGB)
- Text size varies
- Icons vary (skull 💀, crosshair ✝️, weapons 🔫, custom emojis)
- Formatting varies ([PLAYER] killed [PLAYER], PLAYER → PLAYER, PLAYER ☠️ PLAYER)

🔍 WHAT TO DETECT (Pattern Recognition):

**1. KILL EVENTS** - Look for these semantic patterns ANYWHERE on screen:
   - Skull icons: 💀 ☠️ 💥 ⚰️ 
   - Death verbs: killed, wasted, murdered, executed, assassinated, eliminated
   - Weapon names: AK-47, Sniper, RPG, Pistol, Shotgun, SMG
   - Player names with separators: PlayerA → PlayerB, PlayerA killed PlayerB
   - Kill notification boxes/panels (any color/position)

**2. REVIVE/HEAL EVENTS** - Look for:
   - Cross/medical icons: ✝️ ⚕️ 💊 💉 🏥
   - Heal verbs: revived, healed, saved, rescued, resurrected
   - Green/cyan/healing colors in text

**3. DOWN/KNOCKDOWN EVENTS** - Look for:
   - Down icons: ⬇️ 🔻 
   - Down verbs: downed, knocked, incapacitated
   - Yellow/orange warning colors

**4. TEAM/FACTION DETECTION** - Extract from:
   - Clan tags: [BALLAS], [FAMILIES], (VAGOS), {GROVE}
   - Color-coded names (analyze RGB values)
   - Team icons next to player names
   - Matching player name patterns

**5. PLAYER LIST** (usually left side) - Identify:
   - List of player names with health/armor bars
   - Team grouping (players with same icon/color = same team)
   - Alive vs dead status (grayed out, crossed out, skull icon)

🧠 INTELLIGENT EXTRACTION STRATEGY:

Step 1: SCAN ENTIRE SCREEN for text clusters that might be kill feed
Step 2: IDENTIFY SEMANTIC PATTERNS (name + action + name)
Step 3: EXTRACT CONTEXT (emojis, colors, relative positions)
Step 4: GROUP RELATED INFORMATION (player list teams, kill feed events)
Step 5: INFER TEAMS from player list grouping and visual cues
